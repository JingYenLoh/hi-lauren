Introduction Message

Residents,

Congratulations on your choice of residency at The Interlace, a unique home with a distinctive architectural design where urban living meets nature. We have prepared this Resident Guide which provides basic guidelines on the use of recreational facilities and common areas, renovation of your home, house rules, social behaviour and etiquettes, as well as home maintenance tips. The full authority and responsibility for the enforcement of these house rules lie with the Management and it reserves the right to amend the house rules from time to time where deem necessary. To assist you in settling into your new home, we have provided a management team on site to answer any queries you may have. If you have further questions other than those covered in this guide, please feel free to contact the management staff who will be pleased to be of service to you. M/s Ankerite Pte Ltd would like to extend our welcome to you and your family into your new home.
